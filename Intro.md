- broad network access
- resource pooling
- rapid elasticity
- measure service (PAYG)

- Iaas - Infrastructure as a service
- Paas - Platform as a service
- Saas - Software as a service

- Regions contain many zones
- zone -> zone < 5ms
- Multi Region - stored in 2 locations >= 160km apart
